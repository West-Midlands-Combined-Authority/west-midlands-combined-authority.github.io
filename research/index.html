West Midlands Outcomes Framework State of the Region Dashboard

The Inclusive Growth Framework is the basis of the West Midlands Outcomes Framework. This is monitored through an annual State of the Region report setting out the context, trends, activities and outcomes for people and place across the West Midlands over the past twelve months. The underlying data is made available through the West Midlands Outcomes Framework State of the Region dashboard.
